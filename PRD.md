# Lead Magnet Carousel — PRD

## Description

### Brief

NL uses standardised lead magnet landing pages to promote offers and capture inbound enquiries. The goal of this work is to extend the existing lead magnet template by introducing an image carousel that showcases the outcomes or benefits of the offer in a visually engaging way.

The carousel should integrate seamlessly with the currently live lead magnet page, maintaining visual consistency while improving how supporting information and outcomes are presented.

**Reference landing page(s):**
- We'll build you a Power BI report for free | nimblelearn.com

### Scope

- Introduce an image carousel component into the lead magnet template
- Ensure the carousel works and flows well with the existing page structure
- Support the following configurations:
  - No carousel images
  - 3 carousel images
  - More than 3 images (up to ~8)
- To expand on the above layout options, each could be an option whether it supports having no images, 3 images or more than 3 images. The design may mean images have to be sized differently depending on the three scenarios — one size doesn't fit all if we want it to look good
- UX/UI design only (no content creation or development work)
- Open to light improvements to information hierarchy and layout where beneficial

### Acceptance Criteria

**Given** the lead magnet template carousel UX/UI has been designed,
**When** it is reviewed against requirements,
**Then** the following must be true:

- The carousel integrates cleanly with the existing lead magnet page design and flow
- The page remains visually balanced and usable when:
  - No carousel is present
  - A small set of images is provided
  - A larger set of images is provided
- The carousel clearly supports the value of the offer by showcasing outcomes or examples
- The design aligns with NL branding and typography standards
- The carousel does not distract from or compete with the primary call-to-action
- The experience works seamlessly across desktop and mobile
- Navigation controls and interactions are clear and intuitive
- Accessibility and readability standards are met
- The page remains effective as a lead-capture experience with or without the carousel
